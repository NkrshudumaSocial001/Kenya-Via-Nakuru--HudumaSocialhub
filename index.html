About Us

Nakuru Huduma SocialHub is committed to connecting you with key government services quickly and easily. We offer a unique blend of customized and convenient services delivered by the Government of Kenya, both online and offline. We also do land and property consultancy and Sales through our Property Department under Mr. Moses Munene. Our platform ensures seamless access to essential government services, enabling users to engage with them from anywhere, without geographical limitations. Whether it's applying for licenses, accessing social services, or managing taxes, we make it easy for you to interact with the government in a way that suits your needs. In addition to these services, we provide comprehensive civic education on various important topics, including the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and other ongoing flagship projects by the Government of Kenya. Our educational resources aim to equip citizens with knowledge and understanding of governmental initiatives, ensuring informed participation and engagement in national development.Our hybrid service model caters to a wide audience, ensuring accessibility and inclusivity for all citizens, whether they prefer to interact with us digitally or in person. With a focus on empowering citizens through both service delivery and education, we strive to contribute to a more informed, active, and connected society.
